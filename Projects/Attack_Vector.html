Attack_Vector.exe

Information

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

My Responsibilities: Game Design, Graphics, Programming.

Start Date: 10/11/2024

Progress: Ongoing

Team Size: 2

Description: A Metal Gear Solid inspired 3D stealth game, where the player has to sneak past enemies and surveillance cameras to reach the goal undetected.

I programmed features such as the alert system, enemy AI, player movement, graphical shaders, game rules, etc. I am also designing the games levels and entity behaviours. I am working with a musician who handles the music, and when the music plays.

The game is both in English and Japanese as a way to make it more visually interesting and sci-fi.

The final game will have at minimum 10 levels, with each building upon the player's previous experiences in the game.
